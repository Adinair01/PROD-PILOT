
# PROD PILOT
## Pre-Development Decision Intelligence Platform for Product Teams

PROD PILOT is a scalable backend-driven system designed to help product teams evaluate feature feasibility, identify risks, and surface system dependencies **before engineering commitment.**

Modern teams often rely on meetings, fragmented discussions, and tribal knowledge when making product decisions. PROD PILOT introduces a structured intelligence layer that reduces ambiguity and enables faster, data-informed decision-making.
